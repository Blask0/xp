Vision and Values

Our vision and values define a common approach for all our employees stating how we do business at Enonic. They provide a fundamental guide for taking care of our customers. Together they set the standard for how we work in order to create sustainable value for our customers, partners, employees, and shareholders.

Enonic is a dedicated, highly specialized, and innovative software company. Our deep knowledge of customer needs and requirements, as well as long-term user and operational experience, is deployed in our software. Enonic offers the ultimate software platform for business-critical, innovative web solutions based on JAVA technology.

Our vision: Extreme Flexibility

Our goal is to supply a platform and solutions to our customers that offer them extreme flexibility, in scalability, funtionality, and technology.

Our values

Our values are a constant reminder of how we should serve all our customers around the world. They inspire us to be a driving force in modern communications and customer satisfaction.

We want to make it easy for our customers to get what they want, when they want it. We will keep our promises. We will deliver fresh ideas. And, as an international company, we will respect our customers and their local cultures.

Our key values:

  • Safety - We pursue predictability, robustness, and scalability in our staff and in our products
  • Passion - We are dedicated to innovative web solutions and technology
  • Courage - Through challenging conventional solutions and crossing new borders, we drive innovation to solve customer needs

Commitment to our customers

  • Knowledge - We deploy leading-edge technology and maintain an expert focus, fueled by customer needs
  • Flexibility - “Grow as you need” and “pay as you grow” – a technical platform and business model
  • Innovation - We use Java and Open Source core technology, matching business needs with technological opportunities, implementation and operational experience embedded in the platform

Strategy

Our main focus is to develop a world-class software for innovative web solutions based on Java and Open Source technology

We deliver and implement solutions through our extensive partner network with certified and highly skilled professionals
